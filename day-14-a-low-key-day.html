Day 14: A low-key day

Posted on January 24, 2019 by Nathan Backman

[Written by Drew & Kayle]

For the app, Drew and Tyler worked towards getting a recommendation page up and running – by the end of the day, the engine and UI were nearly finished. Kayle worked on a page to check out reviews in their entirety (photo, comment, and user that posted it.) Michael worked further on finishing up error handling and bug fixes, while Nick began to work on the ‘Search by Item’ page.

      

On the API side, Michael fixed some naming issues, did some bug fixes and worked on a route to get reviews by user. Dr. Backman finished a script that allowed dumping our actual data in the app to a different database – allowing us to test with slightly stale production data.

 

As far as the day-to-day, it was a pretty lax. We worked until lunch time before some of the group left for a grocery run. We continued work after lunch, but decided we wanted a bit of free time. After a while, most of us went to find some ice cream. Dr. Backman pointed us toward Molly Moons, a recommendation from esteemed alumni Sarah who spent a summer in Seattle doing an internship at Microsoft. This shop was small, but offered great ice cream and a no tipping policy; Drew was a fan of this. Tyler had one small gripe, but later remedied it. After the ice cream run, more work was done on the app.

      

Following mid-day development, the team walked to dinner. The restaurant in question was Witness, recommended by Garrett as the owner was an old friend of his family. This was a higher-end restaurant focusing on Southern food. Overall opinions of Witness were high. Tyler loved his shrimp and grits with bacon. Michael, Drew, and Garrett enjoyed their chicken and waffles. The rest of team had nothing extreme to say about their dishes. Not previously mentioned in this blog (probably) was that this restaurant was denied earlier in the trip because of its 3 mile total walking distance from our house. After dinner, more work was done concluding this relaxing and productive day.
